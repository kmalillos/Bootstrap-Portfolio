About

Have you ever heard of an operations manager and a wish fulfillment specialist? That's Kaylah. Oh, and she's learning full stack web development.

On a day-to-day basis, Kaylah manages office operations and staff culture working directly with the executive team, as well as coordinates complex, individualized events and experiences for her clients. Kaylah excels at prioritizing and coordinating simultaneous tasks and projects and she thrives in a fast-paced nonprofit/startup environment individually and on a team.

Kaylah enjoys learning and taking on new and exciting challenges. A self-proclaimed techie, Kaylah is currently gaining new technical skills web development in an all-Women's Coding Bootcamp at her alma mater, the beautiful University of Denver.

For fun, Kaylah enjoys binging on some Netflix shows, enjoying the Colorado sunshine, and planning her next adventure. Travel or podcast recommendations? Send her a note!
